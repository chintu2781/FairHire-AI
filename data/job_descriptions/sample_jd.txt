Job Title: Machine Learning Engineer

Job Description:
We are looking for a Machine Learning Engineer who can build intelligent systems
to automate HR and recruitment workflows.

Responsibilities:
- Build NLP and ML models for resume ranking
- Develop and maintain deep learning pipelines
- Deploy models into scalable cloud environments
- Work with cross-functional teams to improve model fairness

Required Skills:
- Python, ML, NLP, Deep Learning
- Experience with TensorFlow or PyTorch
- Knowledge of Transformers (BERT, RoBERTa, DeBERTa)
- Familiarity with REST APIs & deployment

Preferred:
- Experience with HR Analytics
- Experience working with unstructured text data
